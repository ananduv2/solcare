About Us

Found in 2019, SolCare Technologies is a developing IT company with latest technologies and best solutions to serve the purpose of our clients. Equipped with a team of professionals, we aim to provide better result for web development, app development and AI technologies. We also master in AR, VR and MR design models.

“Your potential, Our passion.” At SolCare, we encourage young minds to think out of the box and do something productive for the betterment of the society. Providing our clients with the latest technological outcomes is our driving force. As a growing company, we plan to broaden our scope to various domains to make our results user-friendly.

Improving ourselves and making us better each day is our everyday motto. We believe in making all our dreams into a productive and useful technologies. This makes us unique and different. Every bit of your success is our happiness, so if you have a dream, we are ready to pave the way.
